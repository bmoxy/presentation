    Precision Agriculture

  • Data is already being used heavily within the Agricultural industry, with emphasis on satellite imagery and weather tracking.
  • In the first half of [2015], venture investment in agtech start-ups reached $2.06 billion.2
  • "the use of water and fertilizer will be measured and monitored in detail, sometimes on a plant-by-plant basis." Low-cost sensors placed within fields, or on the plants themselves could return valuable data to increase efieciency and decrease fertilizing

    Disney's MagicBands

  • Wristbands given to every visitor of the Magic Kingdom, that feature a long-range radio that can transmit more than 40 feet in every direction. Used in place of park tickets, credit cards, and hotel keys. Just tap your braclet and you are on your way.
  • Imagine walking into a restaurant at the park and having your meal, that you picked within an app prior to sitting down, delivered to you instantly after sitting down.
  • ...or Snow White being able to find you for a photo, rather than the other way around.3
